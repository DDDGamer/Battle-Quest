Battle Quest

You, so curious and so bold, decide to set out on a journey. You wander around the world looking for an adventure. You see many places, step foot on new lands and learn new things.

Eventually you come to a place where you meet an old man. It is late and you need rest, so you ask him if you can stay for the night. He lets you in his house, gives you some food, and shows you your bed. For some reason, even though you never have seen him before, the old man seems to recognize you.

The next morning the old man tells you that he is a prophet and has foreshadowed your coming...

Controls

Battle Screen

Hp is your Health. Mp is your Magic Points.

When you are hit by your enemy you lose some health depending on the level of your enemy. If both you and your enemy use Block, then both of your Hp are increased depending on the defence power.

When you use Magic to attack, you use up Mp. If you use Melee or Block some Mp is restored.

Select your move in the ComboBox, then confirm your selection by clicking the 'Choose Your Move!' button.

Map Screen

Click on the enemy pictures to battle them. Some enemies will not be available until a lower level enemy is killed.
